Sexual Assaulting (IxD+Installations Design)

"Sexual Assaulting" aims to use the qualities of sweaters and wool as a metaphor for sexual harassment. The action of pulling the wool suggesting sexual harassment and the tattered sweater suggesting the victim. The interactive installation adds interactivity and immersion to the experience, causing people to rethink sexual harassment and understand the victims.

The act of pulling and tugging on the threads of a sweater implies harassment and aggression against a person. When people see the dangling threads of a sweater, they will pull and tug on the threads out of curiosity. Just like many people sexually harass others out of curiosity and impulsiveness. But the act of tugging (sexual harassment) is easily controlled by curiosity, exploration, and impulse, and once it starts, it is hard to stop. Only when the wool is pulled all the way through can it stop, but by this time, a complete sweater has been torn to pieces.
